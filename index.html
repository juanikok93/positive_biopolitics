Biological Backends

Behind the phenotypic front-end of the day-to-day, there exists a rich and expansive biological back-end -- genomic data, bio-markers, pathogenic pasts. How can this back-end be exposed, interfaced, end-pointed to be made functional?

Nested Microbiomes

The data revealed nested microbiomes, akin to Russian dolls. The soil and free-iving samples contained the widest range of microbes. Primary producers—plants and algae—hosted the next greatest range, although just a subset of the diversity seen in the valley environment. The plant and algae eaters had a subset of that subset, and carnivores had the least diverse microbiomes of all...he microbes in the landscape set the stage for those found within hosts. And somehow each organism’s place in the food web helps determine what microbes it acquires.

DNA Barcoding

"DNA Barcoding is a groundbreaking technique used to identify the diversity of species in our ecosystem based on their DNA. It could potentially course-correct human-related land degradation and enable researchers to develop tools to protect ecosystems against loss. The process of collecting and identifying thousands of organisms is tedious and time-consuming for humans. However, machine learning algorithms could significantly reduce the time it takes in a task where every second counts."

Relations and Relata

Rather than to understand the individual as an atomic unit amongst other units, we take the individual as a set of relations amongst other sets of relations, each of which has accrued its own set interactions unfolding over time. In an era of digital payments, we can trace the lineage of people, places, and things through the infrastructure of Point of Sale transactions, reframing relations and intersections in the physical world as a network of contact; a low-fidelity but high-integrity compression of physical movement.

Military Biopolitical Complex

The global tracking systems to detect pathogens and outbreaks were set in place, to certain degree, as mechanisms to contain chemical, biological, and toxin warfare. This shows that long-standing military concerns over bio-warfare can be steered towards appropriate sensing of microbial activity.

Measurement for Bionomics

We have tools for measuring markets, health, and even death. Yet we lack an appropriate framework to quantify the lack of biotic wealth. What is the cost of exosomatised immune technology vs. richer immune defenses derived from a relationship with healthy biological environments? What is the cost of the human on the petri dish, vs. the one rhizomatically embedded into biotic systems?

Telescopes, Microscopes, ___scopes

The scale and focus level of what we account for. They're not only technically differentiated, they demand separate epistemological fidelity resolutions. A positive biopolitics shall necessarily require additional scopes and resolutions to be added and accounted for.

A metabolism of the gut and the table

The biopolitical should extend the governing of human bodies. It should include a true bio-, an account for the microbiota inside and outside our guts, as well as the large bio-energy storage banks we call intensive animal farming. Instead of mere passive reservoirs, these shall be accounted for as a metabolism that find us in the role of intermediary.

Hand of Ulster and Heatmap bio-sovereignty

Ulster’s founding legend has it that the first to touch the land in a boat race claims its sovereignty. Exploiting a loophole, one man bloodily severs his own hand and throws it onto the land, thus claiming the territory. This sovereignty as exuding from the body and the labor of citizens may propose a "Heatmap sovereigty", a real-time activity map of spatial activity that also reflects sovereign claims, not only on behalf of nations, but perhaps on behalf of hemispherical stacks, bioregions, etc.

Pathogen Warfare v Public Sanitation

Public health institutions emerged as response to pathologies in industrialized urban centers. They carried out social campaigns and infrastructural provision of clean water, waste removal, nutrition, workplace safety, etc. With the advent of biotechnology, its focus shifted towards an intervention on specific pathogens. What is the degree of each strategy needed for implementing a positive biopolitical plan?

Participatory self-surveillance

Is crucial to attune the levels of tracking and sensing of epidemiological social status. Mere technical modeling (SIR or SIS or SEIR) do not resemble the intrinsic dynamics of epidemics. Yet, issues of transparency, authoritativeness or suveillance always come to the fore. By opening up the systems for accountability, yet retaining an ultimate layer of anonimity, individuals can be the best sensing and caring layer for the shifting epidemiological reality.

War on Drugs and Bio-Disallowance

Biopolitics is about setting parameters for the allowed and the unaccepted. The war on drugs, although remote in location (and in time, as an inherited policy from Nixon's mandate) from coca producing regions, manifests in glyphosate (Roundup®) fumigation that basically disallows not only the coca plant, but also any other vegetation, microbial, worm or animal that inhabits the ecosystem.
